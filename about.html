About Siren Electric – Licensed Electricians in Bremerton & Kitsap

Siren Electric is a locally owned, family operated business. We take pride in every installation from start to finish. When you call Siren Electric, you are guaranteed a contractor with the professional expertise, integrity, and work ethic needed to complete the job.

At Siren Electric, we believe that great service means more than just flipping a switch—it’s about communication, honesty, and showing up for our community. When you hire Siren Electric, you’re not just hiring an electrician—you’re supporting a local business committed to quality, equity, and doing the job right.

Our Vision

At Siren Electric, our vision is to create a more inclusive, empowered, and electrified future—one project at a time. We strive to be a trusted name in electrical service throughout Kitsap County by delivering high-quality workmanship, building meaningful relationships, and setting a new standard for representation and reliability in the trades. As we grow, we envision a company that not only powers homes and businesses, but also uplifts people—by supporting equity in the trades, creating career opportunities, and showing that great service comes in all forms.
